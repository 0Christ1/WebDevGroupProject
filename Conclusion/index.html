With AI technology becoming more and more integrated with our day to day lives, we should expect to see forms of companionship and social interaction be influenced by those same algorithms. Perhaps social interactions will benefit from more personalized systems, and people suffering from social isolation will find comfort in AI companions. However, it is important to constantly scrutinize and research how those algorithms work, and the kinds of data that fuel them. There is great potential in neural networks, as long as principles of tech-ethics are maintained strictly.
